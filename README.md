### Invoice Database

In response to market changes, the company plans to create a shoe database using their invoice forms. This database will gather transaction details, including shoe models, sizes, colors, prices, and customer information. By analyzing this data, the company aims to identify trends, preferences, and emerging patterns. The database's structure is meticulously designed to ensure data accuracy, with continuous updates to maintain relevance. Ultimately, this initiative underscores the company's commitment to data-driven decision-making, offering insights that will shape product strategies, inventory management, and customer engagement.
